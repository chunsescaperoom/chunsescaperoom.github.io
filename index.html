Why now

This experience was never designed to exist anywhere else.

It was built for a specific physical space — with timing, structure, and environmental details that cannot be copied.

After three years in its current location, that space may no longer be available.

Moving it requires dismantling, transporting, rebuilding, and preserving something fragile through the process.

This is no longer just about preservation.
It is about whether this experience can continue to exist at all.
